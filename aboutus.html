About Us

Vana Koutsomitis

Vana Koutsomitis is a native New Yorker who started her investment banking career in London in 2009 after graduating from Cornell, an elite Ivy League University.

The daughter of a Colombian mother and Greek father, Vana was always passionate about connecting people. In 2012, Vana left finance to start
The City Street, a networking site for financial professionals. The name "The CityStreet" represents her aim: to connect bankers from The City of London to Wall Street in New York.

Vana speaks English, Spanish, Greek, French and Chinese.

Nana Wereko-Brobby

Nana Wereko-Brobby is a writer, matchmaker and founder of London’s invite only social dating club, Social Concierge.

Set up in September 2011, Nana spends her time curating date nights and parties for London’s singles, and is a columnist for Yahoo Lifestyle and regular contributor to national magazines.

Graduating from Edinburgh University, Nana worked in diverse creative fields - from food and drink journalism, to a digital agency in Shoreditch, a global publishing house in New York, and back to London where she found her calling as a ‘date concierge’.
